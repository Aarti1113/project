Our Mission

At Remine India, our mission is to create a sustainable future by recycling e-waste and lithium batteries in an environmentally responsible and socially ethical manner. We aim to reduce the impact of electronic waste on the environment and promote circular economy practices by recovering valuable resources and preventing harmful substances from entering landfills or being illegally dumped.

What We Do

Remine India offers comprehensive e-waste and lithium battery recycling services for individuals, households, businesses, and industries. Our services include collection, transportation, sorting, dismantling, recycling, and disposal of electronic waste and lithium batteries. We ensure that all our processes comply with local regulations and international standards to guarantee safe and environmentally responsible recycling practices.

Why Choose Remine India?

Expertise

Remine India is a recognized startup with a team of experienced professionals who are experts in the field of e-waste and lithium battery recycling. We have the knowledge and technical expertise to handle various types of electronic waste and lithium batteries.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainability by recovering valuable resources from e-waste and lithium batteries, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and minimizing environmental pollution.

Compliance

We strictly adhere to all local regulations and international standards for e-waste and lithium battery recycling. We ensure that our processes are environmentally responsible and socially ethical.
